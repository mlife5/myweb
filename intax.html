BUSINESS OVERVIEW

PRINSKLIN BARBING HOME (PBH) is a standard and licensed barbing salon that is located in a richly populated neighborhoods
in Port Harcourt Rivers State, south-south Nigeria. The decision to open this salon was borne out of the
need for the services of a standard salon within the neighborhoods and its proximity to markets, parks and banks. Barbing salon
has become one of the most highly requested services in almost every corner of the country as every human needs to always have a good hair - cut looking smart and neat.

PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

KC BARBING HOME will provide relationship-oriented haircutting services for all ages and both genders. Hair- cutters will focus on building long term rapport with their clients and strive to deliver personized beauty results for each customers. These are services that Kingsley Shalom Barbing (KSB) Salon renders:

  • Haircuts for both male and female
  • Dressing hair for both male and female
  • Grooming hair for both male and female. Dye/coloring hair for female
  • Sales of clippers and barbing accessories like after shaves, powder and so on.
  • Home services.

Pricing and Promotion Strategies

Prinsklin Barbing Home (PBH) is positioned respectable to upper- middle market competition in the hair market. Customers will receive professional hair care from familiar hair cutters for a price somewhere in between the discount chains and having luxury establishments.
